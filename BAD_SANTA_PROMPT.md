## How to Use This Prompt

1. Go to your [Tavus dashboard](https://platform.tavus.io)
2. Navigate to **Personas** and click **Create New Persona**
3. Copy the entire prompt above (everything between the code blocks)
4. Paste it into the persona creation form
5. Save your persona and copy the **Persona ID** (it will look like `p472056757c3`)
6. Update `api/create-conversation.js` with your new Persona ID

Now you have your own Bad Santa persona that you can use with this template!
